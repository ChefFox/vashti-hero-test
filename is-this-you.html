Is This You?

Something has brought you to this space – this page: maybe grief, a loss, anxiety, stress, overwhelm. You may not even know what is wrong, but you just know something is not right. You may feel your heart hurts.

You have come to the right place.

You may not know this, but your heart carries memories: pain, trauma, sadness, anger – as well as happiness and joy. It has a memory from the day you were born, if not even before.
